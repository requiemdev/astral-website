Frequently Asked

Why Astral?

Due to YouTube shutting down large music bots, we have decided to release Astral Music, a small bot with plenty of features using DisTube's API to provide music for everyone!

Will Astral get shut down?

As of now, no! We are a very small bot, and we do not profit off these bots in any way. This is simply a project for fun and to develop skills!

Will Astral support other sources such as spotify and soundcloud?

Yes! Spotify and Soundcloud integration will be implemented into DisTube V3, where a lot of new features will be introduced.
